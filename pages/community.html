our model how we build lasting change

Inspired by World Vision's 15-year community development model, we work alongside local leaders until communities can sustain progress on their own.

1

listen & assess — years 1-2

We meet with local community leaders, families, and youth to understand critical needs. A majority of our staff are local residents who already understand the community.

2

implement programs — years 2-8

Launch education, mentorship, health, and life-skills programs. Sponsor-child relationships are established. Community members participate in and take ownership of projects.

3

empower & transition — years 8-15

Train local leaders to run programs independently. Community sets its own goals. We step back as the community sustains progress on its own.

self-sustaining community

The community thrives independently. Alumni become mentors. The cycle of poverty is broken for generations.
